Ingridients which you need to make a delicious Pizza-

Ingridients for cheese burst PIZZA

***** With Yeast *****

  • Warm Water - 1/2 cup
  • Sugar - 1 tbsp
  • Yeast - 1 tsp
  • Flour / Maida - 1 1/2 cup
  • Milk Powder - 2 tbsp
  • Salt - 1 Tsp
  • Butter - 1 tbsp
  • Dry Flour / Maida - to help to roll
  • Maize Flour / Makai ka atta - to help to roll
  • Cheese Spread - 3-4 tbsp (any brand, I prefer go)
  • Pizza Sauce - 3 tbsp
  • Pizza Cheese - as desired / 75g
  • Oil - 2 tbsp

Ingridients for cheese burst PIZZA

***** Without Yeast *****

  • Maida - 1 cup
  • Mustard Oil - 1 tbsp
  • Salt - 1 tsp
  • Baking Powder - 1 tsp
  • Baking Soda - ¼ tsp
  • Fresh Yogurt - to make dough (around ⅓ cup)
  • Oil - 2 tbsp
  • Dry Flour - to roll pizza base
  • Pizza Sauce - 6 tbsp
  • Pizza Cheese - 150g
  • Pizza Herbs - 1 tsp

Home Made Pizza Sauce & White Sauce

    Pizza Sauce:-
  • 1/2 Inch Ginger
  • 4-5 Small cloves of garlic
  • 1 Small Onion
  • 1 Small Tomato
  • 1/2 Cup Water
  • 2-3 Tsp Oil
  • 1 Tsp Red chilli powder
  • 1/4 Tsp Oregano
  • 1 Tsp Sugar
  • Salt to taste
    • White Sauce:-
  • Butter- 1 tbsp
  • Maida- 2 tsp
  • Milk- 1.5 cups
  • Chilli flakes- 1 tsp
  • Black pepper powder- 1 tsp
